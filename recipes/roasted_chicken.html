Roasted Chicken

Description

Roasted Chicken is freakingdeliciousevery human being alive should eat Roasted Chicken

Ingridients

  • chicken
  • butter
  • seasoning

Steps

  1. melt the butter
  2. mix the seasoning with the butter
  3. rub the butter and mixed seasoning under the skin of the chicken
  4. put the chicken in the oven
  5. pour the butter and seasoning mix on the chicken every 30 minutes until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 250 degrees
