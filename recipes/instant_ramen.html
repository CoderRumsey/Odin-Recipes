Instant Ramen

Description

Instant Ramen is a quick and delicious meal

Ingridients

  • water
  • ramen packet
  • seasoning
  • sauce

Directions

  1. boil water
  2. add the ramen to the boiled water
  3. once the ramen is cooked put it into a bowl
  4. add the sauce and seasoning to the bowl
  5. stir the ramen until the seasoning and sauce are mixed
