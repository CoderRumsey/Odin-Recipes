Stir Fry

Description

Stir fry is an extremly delicious dish made using a wok

Ingridients

  • noddles
  • oil
  • vegtables
  • protein

Steps

  1. heat the wok up on a stove until the wok appears dry and you see white smoke coming from the pan
  2. add a neutral oil to the wok
  3. use a paper towel to spread the oil
  4. add ingridients in order of how long it will take to cook them
