Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled Cheese is a simple dish that can be made quickly

Ingridients

  • bread
  • cheese
  • mayo
  • oil

Steps

  1. heat up a cast iron pan until the pan appears dry and there is white smoke coming off the pan
  2. add oil to the pan
  3. wipe the pan with a paper towel until the oil is evenly distributed
  4. add the first slice of bread
  5. add cheese
  6. add mayo
  7. add the next slice of bread
  8. after about one minute flip the sandwich
  9. once the cheese is melted remove the sandich
